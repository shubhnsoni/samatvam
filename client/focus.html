Restore Phase

Week 3 Focus

Evening Wind-Down Ritual

Create a 30-minute buffer between your last screen and sleep. Signal to your nervous system that the day is done and it's safe to rest.

Why this matters

Your nervous system doesn't have an off switch — it has a dimmer. Screen light and mental stimulation keep the dimmer turned up. A wind-down ritual gradually turns it down, allowing your body to transition into recovery mode naturally.

What "good enough" looks like

✓ Screens off 30 minutes before bed (most nights)
✓ Dim the lights in your living space
✓ Do something calming: read, stretch, breathe
✓ If you miss a night, just try again tomorrow

Coach tip

"Don't try to build the perfect routine. Just start with one thing: put your phone in another room. Everything else will follow." — Deepti
