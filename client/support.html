Common Questions

How quickly will my coach respond?
WhatsApp: within a few hours during business hours. In-app messages: within 24 hours.
What if I'm struggling with a habit?
Reach out anytime. Struggling is normal and expected. Your coach can help adjust the approach.
Can I reschedule a session?
Yes, with 24 hours notice. Just message your coach on WhatsApp.
Is this a crisis support line?
No. If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a professional helpline. We can provide referrals if needed.
